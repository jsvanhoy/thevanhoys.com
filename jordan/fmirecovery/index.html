Core Values

Foundation Medical, Inc. maintains five (5) core values which help maintain the successfulness of our clinic, your clinic.

RESPECT

RESPECT towards our patients, our colleagues, ourselves and our community.

FOCUS

FOCUS on delivering excellent individualized care and compassion to our patients like we would our own family.

COMMUNICATION

We COMMUNICATE and collaborate because addiction is not a one and done disease it takes all hands-on deck working together to build a foundation for each patient to begin walking towards a life of recovery.

integrity

INTEGRITY in our work, our approach towards patients and each other, always, because integrity is not just what you do in front of others, it is what you do when no one is looking at all that truly matters.

self-improvement

SELF IMPROVEMENT individually and as a team so that we always present our best selves to our patients and because we ask our patients to make changes it is invaluable they see this action in us.
