FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I see a physician during each visit?
Absolutely! All patients will be evaluated, and treated by a physician, at every visit.

Will I ever see a Nurse Practitioner or Physicians Assistant?
No. All patients at Foundation Medical, Inc. Office, will be evaluated, and treated by Licensed Medical Physician, at every visit.

How often do I have to take a urine drug screen?
Initial visit, and then drug testing will be performed according to federal and state guidelines.

Do you prescribe Subutex?
Subutex, which is buprenorphine (bu·pre·nor·phine) without naloxone, will only be prescribed during pregnancy

Does your office prescribe Oxycodone?
No. Physicians of Foundation Medical, Inc. office DO NOT prescribe any class 2 opiate or pain medicine. Examples of class 2 opiates that are NOT prescribed at Foundation Medical, Inc., include:

  • Oxycodone (Percocet)
  • Oxycontin
  • Roxycodone
  • Hydrocodone (Lortab)
  • Opana
  • Morphine

Does your office dispense any controlled medication?
No. Foundation Medical, Inc. Office DOES NOT dispense, or keep any controlled substance on the property.
