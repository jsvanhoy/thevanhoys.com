Refunds or Exchanges

1. All Returns REQUIRE receipts, factory tags and labels.

2. Refunds may be given up to 30 days from date of sale (excludes, but not limited to: firearms, ammo, climbing equipment, electronics and compound bows). –See management for details-

3. No cash refunds on credit card purchases or gift cards. Payments made with a check cannot be refunded within 10 days of the transaction date.

4. All refunds require customer phone number, name, and address.

5. All Christmas returns must be made within 10 days of Christmas, or 30 days of original receipt, and proof of purchase is required.

6. All electronics and stoves are subject, at Mahoney`s discretion, to be processed through the manufacturer’s warranty and may not qualify for refund or exchange.

7. No refunds or exchanges on Final SALE ITEMS, firearms, optics, bows, ammunition, opened tree stands, powder, primers, bullets, load bearing gear, fuel, bathing suits, underwear, or watercraft.

8. Any merchandise purchased with a promotional, in-store gift card must be returned with the gift card for full refund amount. If gift card is not present, the amount will be deducted from refund total.

Special Orders and Layaways

1. A 50% deposit is required for ALL special orders

2. Returned special orders are subject to a 50% restocking fee.

Checks

1. Bounced checks can be paid back within five days of notification from Mahoney’s that it has bounced to avoid a $35 fee. However, we will require a $12.50 fee the bank charges us for running the check twice. After the 5 days, there will be a $35 fee, plus the $12.50 added to the total of the bounced check.

2. Two forms of I.D. and two phone numbers are REQUIRED

3. No second party checks

Warranty

1. Archery strings and cables have NO WARRANTY, implied or in writing.

2. All firearms under warranty will be sent back to the manufacturer for their discretion of approval or repair within 60 days. After the 60 days, customer may be responsible for freight charges to and or from the manufacturer.

3. Firearms are a federally registered item, there are NO EXCHANGES! If a replacement is sent for an item there may be charges incurred to register the paperwork or warranty item.

4. Mahoney’s reserves the right to refer any warranty item through the manufacturer for approval, exchange or credit on that item.
